About Me

Hi, I'm Caelan Ghofrani. I'm a full-stack developer with a strong passion for technology and software engineering. I graduated from the University of Galway with a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics Education. While my course and work placement focused on teaching. I discovered my true passion, lies with Software Development.

Proficient in JavaScript, my skills specialize in front-end web development with a solid foundation in back-end development. I also have knowledge in Python and SQL. I am actively learning and improving on these languages by creating projects of my own, while learning new languages and frameworks.

My final year project is where i showcased most of these skills. I created a web application that acted as a "social media" to post and learn about plants and wildlife. Integrating Map API's, databases and more, this was a big project that I am extremely proud of.

In addition to my technical skills, I believe in the power of collaboration and effective communication. I enjoy working with teams to bring ideas to life and taking insight from others to better my ability.
